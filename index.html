Program Overview

The Training Humans Corporate Wellness Framework™ is a comprehensive, science-backed curriculum designed to empower HR leaders, trainers, consultants, and healthcare professionals to design and lead evidence-based workplace well-being programs.

The 10 Corporate Wellness Pillars

Pillar 1: Physical Wellness

Ergonomics, Desk Fitness, Nutrition, & Preventive Screenings.

Pillar 2: Mental & Emotional Resilience

Burnout Mitigation, Mindfulness, Applied NLP, & Psychological First Aid.

Pillar 3: Occupational Wellness

Work-Life Integration, Time & Energy Allocation, & Purpose Alignment.

Pillar 4: Social & Relational Wellness

Psychological Safety, Team Dynamics, DEIB, & Conflict Resolution.

Pillar 5: Financial Wellness

Personal Financial Literacy, Budgeting, Investment, & Debt Relief.

Pillar 6: Safety & Emergency (Signature)

CPR, AED, Basic Life Support (BLS), Workplace First Aid, & POSH.

Pillar 7: Lifestyle & Habit Wellness

Sleep Hygiene, Habit Loops, Recovery Cycles, & De-addiction.

Pillar 8: Leadership Wellness

Empathetic Leadership, Hybrid Team Care, & Managerial Well-being.

Pillar 9: Environmental Wellness

Biophilic Design, Indoor Air Quality, & Sustainable Workspaces.

Pillar 10: Digital Wellness

Digital Detox, Screen-Time Management, & Unplugging Strategies.
