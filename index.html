THE 10 CORPORATE WELLNESS PILLARS™

Curriculum Deep Dive

Our proprietary curriculum addresses every dimension of organizational health, bridging physical safety, mental resilience, digital balance, and leadership culture.

Pillar 1: Physical Wellness

Workplace Ergonomics, Posture Correction, Nutrition, Hydration, Desk Movement, & Preventive Health Screenings.

Pillar 2: Mental & Emotional Resilience

Burnout Mitigation, Applied Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), Stress Physiology, & Psychological First Aid.

Pillar 3: Occupational Wellness

Work-Life Integration, Time & Energy Allocation, Career Stagnation Recovery, & Overload Prevention.

Pillar 4: Social & Relational Wellness

Psychological Safety in Teams, Empathetic Communication, Conflict Resolution, & DEIB Integration.

Pillar 5: Financial Wellness

Personal Financial Literacy, Emergency Fund Planning, Debt Reduction Protocols, & Tax/Retirement Basics.

Pillar 6: Safety & Emergency (Signature)

CPR, Automated External Defibrillator (AED) Operations, Basic Life Support (BLS), First Aid, & POSH Compliance.

Pillar 7: Lifestyle & Habit Wellness

Sleep Hygiene, Circadian Rhythm Alignment, Science of Habit Loops, & Recovery Cycles.

Pillar 8: Leadership Wellness

Compassionate Leadership, Hybrid Team Care, Managerial Distress Detection, & Well-being KPIs.

Pillar 9: Environmental Wellness

Biophilic Office Design, Indoor Air Quality, Workspace Ergonomics/Lighting, & Eco-Sustainability.

Pillar 10: Digital Wellness

Digital Detox Protocols, Screen-Time Fatigue Mitigation, Cyber-Stress Balance, & Off-Hours Boundaries.
